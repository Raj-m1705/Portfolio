About

I’m Rajkumar Meenakshisundaram

I am a finance and shared services transformation leader with over 28 years of global experience across logistics, banking, oil & gas, IT, and consulting.

My journey has taken me from leading finance teams in operations and shared services in multinationals like Cognizant, Shell, Standard Chartered, Maersk, and Agility, to advising organizations as a transformation consultant, fractional CFO, and board-level partner. Along the way, I’ve learned one truth: shared services and finance are no longer just about efficiency, they must enable business strategy.

I specialize in helping organizations turn around finance function, business operations and Global Capability Centers (GCCs) elevating them from transactional delivery to value-adding business partners gaining a seat at the table.
